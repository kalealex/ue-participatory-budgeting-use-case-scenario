49th Ward Participatory Budgeting

2022 / 2023 cycle

How were the project proposals selected?

All listed projects were suggested and vetted by 49th Ward residents. From August to September 2022, community representatives met regularly in committees to determine which project proposals to place on this year’s ballot.

Note: Participation in the 49th Ward participatory budgeting process as a community representative and/or as an attendee at a neighborhood assembly was entirely voluntary and open to all 49th Ward residents, regardless of citizenship or voter registration.
